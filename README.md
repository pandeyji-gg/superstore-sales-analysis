# Superstore Sales Analysis

## What this project does

Analyzes 9,800 rows of US retail sales data from a superstore operating across four regions between 2015 and 2018. The goal was to identify top performing regions and product categories, drill into what drives Technology sales, and uncover monthly trends to understand how the business is growing over time.

## Key Findings

**West region leads in total sales at $710,000**, with South being the weakest at $389,000 — nearly half the West's performance. This gap suggests either a smaller customer base or untapped market opportunity in the South.

**Technology is the highest grossing category at $827,000**, but the real insight is what drives it — Phones alone account for $327,000, nearly double the next sub-category (Machines at $189,000). If the store wants to grow Technology revenue, phones are the lever to pull.

**Sales are growing year over year.** The monthly trend chart shows a clear upward pattern from 2015 to 2018, with end-of-year peaks each year — likely driven by holiday season demand. Each year's peak is higher than the last, which signals healthy business growth.
